## Advice dump

- [ ] Find a color palette and stick to it
- [ ] Make use if natural light
- [ ] Add some textiles to the room (carpet, curtains, drapes)
- [ ] At least 3 other sources of light in a room (exclusing the overhead)
- [ ] Add a rug to the floor to add color, pattern, personality. BEst case rug has 3 colors (2 main and an accent color). IDeally rug colors are similar to walls. No small rug.
- [ ] Most rooms have too many 90 deg angles. Try to bring in some organic shapes and patterns
- [ ] Move things up from the ground. Add some negative space. Don't make the place feels like it's sucked down by gravity.
- [ ] Add color via things that can be moved around (pillows, art, frames, lamps, accesorries, blankets)
- [ ] Posters could be related to interests, but make sure it takes a step of decoringm don't e too on the nose. Search for "<interest> graphic art" on etsy
